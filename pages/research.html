My Research Study

Research Topic

Improving the college experience and and outcomes of first-generation college students through participation in high-impact education practices.

  1. I am a first-generation college graduate.
  2. First-gen students represent 30% to 50% of all college students in the United States.
  3. Research shows that, as a group, first-gen students are more likely to take longer to graduate or not finish their college programs.
  4. Through my research, I want to learn how the University can improve the experiences and the outcomes of first-gen college students.

My Research Plan

Throughout the Spring, Summer, and Fall of 2025, I plan to connect with first-generation students enrolled at University of the Pacific through focus groups, and online survey, and individual interviews over Zoom. I want to gather the voices, experiences, and insights of what it's like to be a first-gen student at Pacific and use that knowledge to recommend how the University might be able to more effectively provide support for helping more have positive learning, social experiences, and continue their efforts to graduate on time.

If you are a full-time, first-generation student at University of the Pacific, please consider lending your voice to this study by sending me a message through my "Contact" page.
