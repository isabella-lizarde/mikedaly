Hi, I'm Mike!

I'm a 3rd year doctoral student here at University of the Pacific and I also work in the Graduate Admissions Office. I have created this website to share a little about myself and my research project on first-generation students attending UOP.

I'm a proud Californian and an even prouder first-gen college graduate. Everyday I'm reminded in some way about how a college education opened many doors and allowed me so many opportunities to both give back and pay it forward.
I also clearly remember my experiences as a new first-gen student struggling to successfully navigate the college landscape, balance work and school, and figuring out how to pay for all of my school and living expenses. It's from this experience and my desire to make it better for today's first-gen students that I chose to pursue my research study. Click on the "Research" tab above to learn more.
