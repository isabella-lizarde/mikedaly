Dissertation Research

Header

I am a first-gen college graduate researching how the university can help elevate our first-year, first-gen students' sense of belonging, connection to the campus, and their learning.

I am seeking first-year, first-gen students, and those who have completed their first year, to participate in a either a confidential focus group, interview, or online survey during the Spring 2025 semester. My goal is to learn about their experiences - the good, and the not-so-good.
